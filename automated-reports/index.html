No mystery. When your report runs, this is what you (and anyone you choose) get — usually as an email, or dropped into Slack or a Google Doc if you’d rather:

  • A plain-English summary at the top — two or three sentences that say what happened: “Revenue was $18,400 last week, up 12% from the week before. Two invoices are still unpaid. Your best channel was referrals.”

The AI writes the words; I build and check the machine that feeds it, so the summary is always right, not just fluent.
